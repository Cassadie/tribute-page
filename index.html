Stevie Nicks

The Reigning Queen of Rock 'n' Roll

Nicks joined Fleetwood Mac in 1975 along with her then-boyfriend Lindsey Buckingham. She began her solo career in 1981 and reached multiplatinum status with the album Bella Donna. Nicks was named one of the Top 100 Songwriters of All Time by Rolling Stone. She is the only woman to have been inducted twice into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, as a member of Fleetwood Mac in 1998 and as a solo artist in 2019. She has garnered eight Grammy Award nominations and two American Music Award nominations as a solo artist. Her lyrics brought a poetic mystery to rock and roll at the time, and paved the way forward for female songwriters yet to come.
